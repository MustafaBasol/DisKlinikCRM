# Product Brief

## Product Name

Health CRM MVP

## One-Sentence Description

A lightweight CRM and clinic operations platform for health centers and dental clinics to manage patients, appointments, follow-ups, treatment opportunities, and payments.

## Problem

Small and medium-sized clinics often manage appointments, patient follow-ups, treatment offers, and payments using disconnected tools such as paper notes, Excel files, WhatsApp messages, and basic calendars.

This causes:

- Missed appointments
- Poor patient follow-up
- Lost treatment opportunities
- Unclear payment tracking
- Lack of visibility for clinic owners
- Operational confusion between doctors, receptionists, and billing staff

## Solution

Create a simple web-based CRM that centralizes:

- Patient records
- Appointments
- Follow-up tasks
- Service/treatment pipeline
- Payment tracking
- Reminder communication
- Clinic dashboard

## Primary Target Market

The first target market should be dental clinics.

Reason:

Dental clinics have clear operational pain points:

- High appointment volume
- No-show problems
- Treatment plans and quotes
- Follow-up needs
- Partial payments
- Recurring controls
- Multiple staff roles

## Secondary Markets

- Health centers
- Aesthetic clinics
- Physiotherapy centers
- Private doctors
- Small specialist clinics

## MVP Success Criteria

The MVP is successful if a small clinic can use it daily to:

- Add patients
- Schedule appointments
- See daily agenda
- Track appointment status
- Send or prepare reminders
- Track follow-up tasks
- Track treatment/service opportunities
- Track simple payments
- View a basic dashboard

## Product Positioning

This is not a full medical software.

This is a CRM and operations tool for clinics.

## Main Differentiators

### 1. No-Show Reduction

The system should help clinics reduce missed appointments through reminders and no-show tracking.

### 2. Treatment / Service Follow-Up

The system should help clinics follow up with patients who received a quote or treatment proposal.

### 3. Simple Clinic Dashboard

Clinic owners should immediately see daily and weekly business activity.

### 4. Easy Adoption

The MVP should be simple enough for receptionists and doctors to use without heavy training.
