# Health CRM MVP

Health CRM MVP is a lightweight customer relationship and clinic operations management system for health centers, dental clinics, and appointment-based care providers.

## Purpose

The goal is to help clinics manage:

- Patients
- Appointments
- Follow-ups
- Treatment/service opportunities
- Payments
- Reminder messages
- Daily operations

The MVP is intentionally focused on CRM and operations, not on full medical record management.

## Target Customers

Initial target customers:

- Dental clinics
- Private health centers
- Aesthetic clinics
- Physiotherapy centers
- Small medical practices
- Multi-practitioner appointment-based clinics

## Core MVP Features

### Patient Management

- Create and manage patient profiles
- Store contact information
- Track patient source
- View patient appointment history
- Add internal notes

### Appointment Management

- Create, update, cancel, and reschedule appointments
- Daily and weekly calendar views
- Appointment status tracking
- Practitioner-based appointment filtering

### Follow-Up Tasks

- Create tasks linked to patients
- Assign tasks to team members
- Track pending, completed, and overdue tasks

### Treatment / Service Pipeline

- Track patient service opportunities
- Manage treatment or service stages
- Track potential revenue
- Follow up on pending offers

### Payment Tracking

- Track simple payments
- Record total, paid, and remaining amount
- Track payment status

### Reminder System

- Send appointment reminders
- Use SMS, WhatsApp, or email-ready templates
- Avoid sensitive medical details in reminder messages

### Dashboard

- Today's appointments
- Weekly appointment count
- No-show count
- New patients
- Pending follow-ups
- Pending payments
- Open treatment opportunities

## Non-MVP Features

The following features are not included in the first MVP:

- Prescription management
- Medical diagnosis
- Full medical history records
- Laboratory integrations
- Insurance integrations
- Patient portal
- Medical imaging storage
- AI diagnosis
- Complex accounting

## Compliance Notes

This system may process sensitive personal data. Security, access control, audit logs, and GDPR-friendly data handling should be considered from the beginning.

For production use in healthcare contexts, hosting and compliance requirements must be reviewed according to the target country.
